BOBCIO & FLACZEK

TRIBUTE TO THE TWO CUTEST CATS

BY ZAZU - A CRAZY CAT LADY (AND PROUD OF IT!)

BOBCIO

  • FOUND ON THE STREET SITTING WITHOUT HIS CAT MUMMY
  • LIVED IN 6 DIFFERENT HOUSES
  • TRAVELED THROUGH MOST OF THE EUROPE
  • FOUND A LOVING FAMILY AND HAS BEEN WITH THEM EVER SINCE
  • GOT LOST AND LIVED ON THE STREETS FOR 6 WEEKS
  • LOVES TO HAVE MEOWING CONVERSATIONS WITH HIS PARENTS
  • LOVES TO BE CUDDLED (BUT ONLY WHEN HE IS IN THE MOOD!)
  • MADE HIDING IN ALL SORT OF BOXES HIS MAIN HOBBY
  • LOOKS LIKE A PANTHER!

FLACZEK

  • ADOPTED FROM STOKE-ON-TRENT
  • MADE FRIENDS WITH HIS NEW BROTHER STRAIGHT AWAY
  • BECAME A LITTLE RASCAL WHO LOVES TO PLAY
  • WAKES HIS MUM UP EVERY MORNING BY STANDING ON HER CHEST AND MEOWING LIKE A LITTLE DEVIL
  • SITS ON HIS PARENTS LAP EVERY TIME THEY FEEL SAD, ANXIOUS OR NERVOUS
  • LOVES TO JUMP OVER THE FENCE TO MEET HIS CAT NEIGHBOURS
  • FALLS ASLEEP IN EVERY POSITION POSSIBLE IN ALL SORT OF PLACES
  • LOOKS LIKE A TIGER!
